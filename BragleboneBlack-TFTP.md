### U-Boot tftp on Beaglebone black

login into u-boot by press any key after power on a board.
```sh
setenv ipaddr 10.42.0.107
setenv serverip 10.42.0.1
setenv ethaddr 12:34:56:ab:cd:ef
setenv autostart no
saveenv
```
then, restart a board
